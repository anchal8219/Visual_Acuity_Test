Myopia

Myopia is an eye defect or common abnormality of the eye in which the near vision is clear while distant vision is blurred.
This condition is known as myopia also it is called near or short-sightedness. Retina is that part of the eye which provides a surface for image formation.
In myopia what happens is light rays entering the eyes converge too soon and are brought to focus before reaching the retina hence the image cannot be formed on the retina.



Symptoms

If a person is myopic he or she will not be able to see the road signs and distant objects clearly but the nearer object will be visible to him/her clearly.
